What Is 4 Point Perspective?

How 4 Point Perspective Works

Unlike traditional linear perspective, 4 point perspective bends vertical lines slightly toward additional vanishing points. This creates a curved visual field similar to wide camera lenses.

  • Two vanishing points control horizontal depth
  • Two vanishing points control vertical distortion
  • Ideal for large-scale environments
